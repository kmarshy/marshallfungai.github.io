(ME) ? "Who Am I" : "Where I came from"

My full name is Fungai Marshall Mangondoza. I was born and raised in Zimbabwe, a country in Southern Africa. This is where I spent most of my junior studies. In 2009 I was enrolled at Cyprus International University in Cyprus, as the name implies. In my second year I transferred to Eastern Mediterranean University also in Cyprus. On the 17th of February 2015, I managed to graduate with a Bsc in Management Information Systems, having a CGPA of 2.9/4.

My work life started immediately after getting an opening as a full time web developer at a Digital Agency called Ata Bilisim. The company gives the developer full control over a project from design to deployment, and for 3 years, my experience and skills as a professional web developer have incredibly increased.

I used to code directly into themes for clients but very recently I started making plugins because I realised I need to share some of those snippets of code with myself in the future and brag of about some tricks I discover. Please visit my github page.
